Jharkhand Mukti Morcha (JMM)

Shibu Soren

Shibu Soren (born 22 January 1944) is an Indian politician and the founding member of the Jharkhand Mukti Morcha (JMM). He is a former Chief Minister of Jharkhand and has played a significant role in the state’s struggle for autonomy and development. Known as the "Guruji" of JMM, he is a prominent tribal leader and has served as a Member of Parliament several times.

Hemant Soren

Hemant Soren (born 10 August 1975) is an Indian politician and the current Chief Minister of Jharkhand. He is the son of Shibu Soren, the founder of Jharkhand Mukti Morcha (JMM). Hemant Soren has played an important role in the state's political landscape and is known for his focus on tribal welfare, education, and healthcare in Jharkhand.

Satyanand Jha (Satyanand Jha 'Satyu')

Satyanand Jha, popularly known as Satyu, is an influential member of the Jharkhand Mukti Morcha. He has been a part of several important decisions and is known for his commitment to the cause of tribal communities and their upliftment.

Ranchi Member of JMM

Ranchi, the capital of Jharkhand, has witnessed significant leadership from the JMM. Madhusudan Mistry has played a role in promoting the values of JMM through his various political initiatives, strengthening the party's influence in Jharkhand.

Sushil Maurya

Sushil Maurya is another key leader in the JMM, representing the party on a national level. His involvement in the party's leadership circles has helped further the cause of JMM, particularly in policy development related to tribal welfare.
