Registered Political Parties of India

Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP)

Right-wing party promoting nationalism, economic development and strong defense policies.
Indian National Congress (INC)

Oldest political party advocating secularism, democratic socialism, and inclusive growth.
Aam Aadmi Party (AAP)

Focused on anti-corruption, education, healthcare and clean governance initiatives.
Jharkhand Mukti Morcha (JMM)

Regional party mainly based in Jharkhand advocating tribal rights, local governance, and socio-economic justice.
Bahujan Samaj Party (BSP)

Advocates for the rights of Dalits, OBCs, and marginalized communities. Strong presence in Uttar Pradesh.
Shiv Sena

A regional party from Maharashtra with a strong focus on Marathi pride, Hindutva ideology, regional development, and cultural preservation.
