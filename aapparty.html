Aam Aadmi Party (AAP)

Arvind Kejriwal

Arvind Kejriwal (born 16 August 1968) is an Indian politician and the current Chief Minister of Delhi. He is the founding member of the Aam Aadmi Party (AAP), which emerged from the India Against Corruption movement. Kejriwal is known for his anti-corruption stance and his efforts in improving education, healthcare, and public services in Delhi.

Manish Sisodia

Manish Sisodia (born 5 January 1972) is an Indian politician and the current Deputy Chief Minister of Delhi. He is a senior leader of the Aam Aadmi Party and holds significant influence in shaping the party's policies, especially in education and governance. Sisodia has been instrumental in the reforms of Delhi's education system.

Atishi Marlena

Atishi Marlena (born 8 June 1981) is an Indian politician and a member of the Aam Aadmi Party. She is known for her work in the education and healthcare sectors in Delhi. Atishi has been actively involved in advocating for reforms in public education and improving the governance of the capital.

Raghav Chadha

Raghav Chadha (born 11 November 1988) is an Indian politician and a prominent member of the Aam Aadmi Party. He is known for his role in the party's expansion and his advocacy on issues such as education, health, and economic reform. Chadha is also a member of the Rajya Sabha, representing Punjab.

Bhagwant Mann

Bhagwant Mann (born 17 October 1973) is an Indian politician and the Chief Minister of Punjab. He was elected as a Member of Parliament and is known for his work in Punjab. Bhagwant Mann's AAP government in Punjab focuses on issues like agriculture, education, and improving the state's infrastructure.
