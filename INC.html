Rahul Gandhi

Rahul Gandhi (born 19 June 1970) is an Indian politician and the current Vice President of the Indian National Congress. He has served as the Member of Parliament for Wayanad, Kerala. Rahul is known for his focus on youth empowerment, education, and agricultural reforms. He is the son of Rajiv Gandhi and Sonia Gandhi, both prominent figures in Indian politics.

Sonia Gandhi

Sonia Gandhi (born 9 December 1946) is an Italian-born Indian politician and the longest-serving president of the Indian National Congress. She has been the leader of the INC for many years and was the United Progressive Alliance (UPA) chairperson, playing a significant role in India's political landscape, especially in the early 21st century.

Priyanka Gandhi Vadra

Priyanka Gandhi Vadra (born 12 January 1972) is an Indian politician and a prominent member of the Gandhi family. She is known for her strong leadership and involvement in the political process, particularly in Uttar Pradesh. She has focused on women’s rights and social justice issues, and is seen as one of the Congress Party’s rising stars.

Manmohan Singh

Manmohan Singh (born 26 September 1932) is an Indian economist and politician who served as the 14th Prime Minister of India from 2004 to 2014. As Prime Minister, he is credited with driving India's economic reforms and liberalization, positioning the country as a global economic power. He was also India's Finance Minister from 1991 to 1996.

Ashok Gehlot

Ashok Gehlot (born 3 May 1951) is an Indian politician and the current Chief Minister of Rajasthan. He has held the position multiple times and is known for his work in social welfare, health, and education. Gehlot is a senior leader of the Congress Party and has been instrumental in shaping the party’s strategies in Rajasthan.
