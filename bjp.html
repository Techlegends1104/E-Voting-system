Narendra Modi

Narendra Damodardas Modi (born 17 September 1950) is an Indian politician who is the 16th and current Prime Minister of India, in office since May 2014. As a child, Modi helped his father sell tea at the Vadnagar railway station, and later ran a tea stall with his brother near a bus terminus. Modi became an RSS pracharak in 1978 and worked in Delhi for RSS from 1979.

Amit Shah

Amit Anil Chandra Shah (born 22 October 1964) is an Indian politician currently serving as the President of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and as a member of the Rajya Sabha. He was elected as an MLA from Sarkhej in four consecutive elections: 1997, 1998, 2002, and 2007. He is a close associate of Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

Nirmala Sitharaman

Nirmala Sitharaman (born 18 August 1959) is an Indian politician and the current Finance Minister of India. She is a member of the Rajya Sabha, representing the state of Karnataka. Sitharaman is known for her work in economic reforms and has served as the Defense Minister of India, making her one of the most prominent women leaders in the country.

Rajnath Singh

Rajnath Singh (born 10 July 1952) is an Indian politician and the current Home Minister of India. He has also served as the President of the Bharatiya Janata Party. Singh has held several important positions in the government, including the Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh, and is recognized for his role in strengthening internal security and national defense.

Smriti Irani

Smriti Zubin Irani (born 23 March 1976) is an Indian politician, television actress, and former model. She is the current Minister of Women and Child Development and also the Minister of Textiles in India. Irani is known for her strong leadership and her tenure as the Minister of Human Resource Development.
